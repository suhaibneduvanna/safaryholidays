HYDERABAD

2 NIGHTS – 3 DAYS
DAY 01:- Hyderabad

- Our executive will meet at 6 AM & welcome you at Cochin airport.
- Proceed to HYD airport at 8 AM from COK airport.
- AC cab arranged for the Airport Pickup & Sightseeing.
- At 10.30 AM will proceed to hotel, on the way will stop for breakfast own cost.
- Early check will provide as per availability
- 12 PM will proceed to Hyderabad sightseeing.
Charminar, Macca Masjid, Chowmahala Palace, Golconda Fort, Ntr Garden, Birla Temple, Gautama Buddha Statue In Hussain Sager Lake Boating, Lumbini Park Evening back to hotel
Overnight Stay at Hotel with Dinner & next day Breakfast


DAY 02:- Ramoji Film City Day Tour

Morning after Breakfast Proceed To Ramoji Film City Tour Holiday Carnival Day Tour 9.00 Am To 8.00 Pm. Guided Tour Of Ramoji Film City in Non-A/C Vintage Bus (10am To 5pm).Visit To Bauble Set (Last Bus At 4pm). Visit to Eco Zone – Butterfly Park, Exotic Bird Park – Wings & Bonsai Garden. Fundustan (Child Play Area), Borasura, Toyland & Rain Dance. Ramoji Movie Magic - Action Theatre & Space Yatra Entertainment Shows At Eureka - Spirit Of Ramoji, Fort Frontier Wild West Stunt Show, Dada Jinn’s Live Tv Show & Lights Camera Action (Hollywood Black Light Spectacular Show). Complimentary Rides - Ranger, Break Dance, Twister, Super Jet, Carousel, Frisbee Coaster. Shooting Locations & Gardens. Live Entertainment Stage Shows At Eureka. Spectacular Carnival Parade. Experience Ramie Film City in Dazzling Lights. Dslr Cameras Above 18-55mm Is Not Permitted And Evening Back to hotel.
Overnight Stay at Hotel with Dinner & next day Breakfast.


DAY 03:- Departure

After a healthy breakfast, checkout of hotel, transfers back to Hyderabad Airport.
