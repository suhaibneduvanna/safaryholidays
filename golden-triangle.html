GOLDEN TRIANGLE

DELHI – JAIPUR – AGRA (4 NIGHTS – 5 DAYS)
DAY 01:- Arrival at Delhi

- Our executive will meet & welcome you on arrival.
- AC Innova Crysta arranged for the Airport Pickup.
- From Airport will proceed to Delhi Hotel
- Delhi Local sightseeing
Over Night stay at Delhi Hotel with Dinner & Breakfast


DAY 02:- Delhi Sightseeing

After breakfast & check out will proceed to Delhi sightseeing
- India Gate
- Humayun's Tomb
- Rashtrpati Bhavan
- Red Fort
- Akshardham Temple
- Dilli Haat INA
- Qutub Minar Complex
- Chandni Chowk Market
Evening travel to Agra & overnight stay in Agra hotel with Dinner & Breakfast.


DAY 03:- Agra Sightseeing

After breakfast proceed to Taj Mahal , Agra fort . Afternoon proceed to Fatehpur Sikri
Evening Travel to Jaipur, overnight stay in Jaipur hotel with dinner & Breakfast.


DAY 04:- - Jaipur Sightseeing

- Amber Fort
- Jal Mahal
- Jantar Mandar
- Hawa Mahal
Overnight stay in Jaipur hotel with Dinner & Breakfast.


DAY 05:- Jaipur Sightseeing – Departure

After Breakfast Proceed to Jaipur City Tour, Shopping and evening drop at Jaipur airport
