KULLU MANALI

(4 NIGHTS – 5 DAYS)
DAY 01:- Delhi To Manali

Arrive in Delhi by Air or Train. You have to reach the boarding point of you own. Bus Departs from Delhi around 6 pm. Reporting time is 5:30pm near ISBT. We will provide you the office / boarding address on the ticket after confirmation. Travel overnight by AC Volvo Video Coach from Delhi to Manali. On the way bus stops at Karnal / Ambala for Dinner (Of your own).

DAY 02:- Manali

Manali is a resort town nestled in the mountains of the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh near the northern end of the Kullu Valley in the Beas River Valley. It is located in the Kullu district, about 270 km (168 mi) north of the state capital, Shimla, 309 km (192 miles) north east of Chandigarh and 544 km (338 miles) northeast of Delhi, the federal capital. The small town, with a population of 8,096 is the beginning of an ancient trade route to Ladakh and from there over the Karakoram Pass on to Yarkand and Khotan in the Tarim Basin. It is a popular tourist destination and serves as the gateway to Lahaul and Spiti district as well as Leh.

Manali Local Sightseeing
Reaches Manali around 7/8 am. You can call up the transporter 10 mins before reaching Manali for pick up. Reaches hotel and Check in. After rest for 2 / 3 Hours you can enjoy Afternoon half day city tour visiting Hadimba Devi Temple, Club House and Tibetan Monastery, etc. Cab will drop you at Hotel / You can take drop at Mall Road if you want to enjoy the shopping and get back to hotel by Auto Rickshaw / Cab.
Dinner & Overnight at hotel.


DAY 03:- Solang Valley- Manali

After breakfast proceed on a day excursion to the Rohtang Pass / Solang Valley – enjoy the beauty of year round snow caped mountains. The rest of the day is free to explore this picturesque town on your own. Evening free for leisure & enjoy your dinner at Hotel.
Overnight stay at hotel in Manali.


DAY 04:- Manali – Kullu – Delhi

After Breakfast Morning excursion to Kullu Rafting Point. Enjoy Rafting, Vaishno Mata temple, Kullu Shawl Emoporiums. In evening travel by AC Volvo buses from Manali to Delhi full night.


DAY 05:- Departure

Reaches Delhi at around 7 am.
